# 09 — Portfolio Reporting & Analytics

**Lifecycle stage 09.** Detailed workflow: [`/03-workflows/09-portfolio-reporting-analytics.md`](../03-workflows/09-portfolio-reporting-analytics.md).

## Purpose

Cross-project visibility for Directors and the FD. Leading indicators: overdue RFIs, open variations awaiting approval, unvalued work done, inspections missed, defects backlog, aged debt, subcontractor exposure. Win/loss and bid-hit analytics on the sales side. Margin leakage by project, client, estimator, package, trade and cause.

## Key concepts at this stage

Portfolio Snapshot, Leading Indicator, Risk Score, Margin Trace.
