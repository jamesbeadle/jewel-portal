# P05 — Site Manager

**Type:** Internal field
**Reports to:** Project Manager
**Frequency on platform:** Daily — on site, mobile-first
**Status:** Draft

## What Site Manager owns

The operational bridge between office and workface. Not a daily-progress data entry role — the live owner of site delivery.

- Day-to-day site execution.
- Trade coordination and sequencing.
- Site readiness and lookahead planning.
- Site diary and oversight of progress capture.
- Quality checks on live work; snagging coordination.
- Day-to-day enforcement of RAMS and site rules.
- Escalation of delays, RFIs, access issues, variation triggers.
- Confirmation that H&S items supplied by H&SO are physically in place on site.

## What Site Manager does **not** own
- The H&S framework or audit regime — that's H&SO.
- The commercial position of variations — that's QS.
- Administrative compliance records — that's OCC.

## Goals
- Have one mobile-first daily control screen that shows everything that matters on this site today.
- Always work to the latest drawing — no chance of working from a superseded version.
- Capture progress, issues and quality checks against the right area / package / cost code, fast.
- Be able to raise an issue (RFI, variation trigger, safety observation) in seconds, not paragraphs.

## Pain points (current state)
- No single live-site dashboard — information scattered across WhatsApp, email, Dashpivot, paper.
- No formal mobilisation gate; site can "start" without RAMS, inductions or welfare being signed off.

## Involvement across workflows
- **Owner on:** 06 (Site Delivery, Programme & Reporting — the live-site slice).
- **Co-owner on:** 04 (H&S Site Mobilisation & Compliance — physical confirmation on site; H&SO owns the framework).
- **Contributor on:** 01 (acknowledges current drawing revision on site), 05 (raises RFIs / variation triggers / NoDs from site), 08 (snag identification and coordination at close-out).
- **Read on:** 03 (knows which subcontractors are awarded to their site), 09 (project-level dashboards).

## Permissions (coarse)
- Write on site diary, progress capture, snags, mobilisation checklist items.
- Approver on subcontractor toolbox-talk attendance, on-site H&S confirmations, snag ownership.
- Read drawings, BoQ sections, RFIs and compliance status for their assigned project.

## Devices
- Phone primarily. Touch-friendly, offline-tolerant. Tablet for inspections.
