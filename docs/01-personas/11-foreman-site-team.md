# P11 — Foreman / Site Team

**Type:** Internal field
**Reports to:** Site Manager
**Frequency on platform:** Daily — on site, mobile-first
**Status:** Draft

## What Foreman / Site Team owns

The workface layer. Distinct from Site Manager (live-site delivery owner). This is the data-capture role at the coal face.

- Workface supervision.
- Daily progress input.
- Photos.
- Labour / attendance capture support.
- Housekeeping / branding standards.
- Immediate issue reporting up to the Site Manager.

## What Foreman / Site Team does **not** own
- Trade coordination or sequencing — Site Manager.
- Sign-off on snags, RAMS enforcement decisions, or escalations — Site Manager.
- Commercial decisions — PM / QS.

## Goals
- Capture daily progress, photos and issues fast.
- Have the current drawing on the phone without asking.
- Flag a problem and have it reach the Site Manager without writing a paragraph.

## Pain points (current state)
- Information lives across WhatsApp, Photos and inboxes.
- Photos that should be evidence end up untagged in camera rolls.

## Involvement across workflows
- **Owner on:** none (data capture, not workflow ownership).
- **Contributor on:** 02 (walk-round capture during pre-construction), 06 (daily progress capture, photos, attendance), 07 (own day timesheets allocated to cost codes), 08 (defect identification).
- **Read on:** 01 (current drawing revision on mobile).

## Permissions (coarse)
- Write on progress, photos, attendance, own timesheets, snag reports.
- Read drawings and BoQ sections for the project.

## Devices
- Phone primarily. Touch-friendly, offline-tolerant.
