# P06 — Health & Safety Officer (H&SO)

**Type:** Internal
**Reports to:** Directors / MD
**Frequency on platform:** Daily — H&S dashboard plus targeted inspections / audits
**Status:** Draft

## What H&SO owns

The H&S framework and assurance for the business. Distinct from OCC (admin/compliance records) and from Site Manager (physical enforcement on site).

- H&S framework and standards.
- Audit regime and inspection templates.
- Incident, near-miss and observation governance.
- Corrective action oversight.
- Mobilisation requirements from the H&S side (RAMS, permits, temporary works, fire / welfare / plant controls).
- Assurance that site controls are planned and evidenced.
- Coaching and escalation on unresolved H&S matters.

## What H&SO does **not** own
- Daily physical running of the site — that's Site Manager.
- General office admin or document filing — that's OCC.
- Commercial valuation decisions — those are QS and PM.

## Goals
- Run the H&S regime as a connected system, not a folder of templates.
- Have a clean separation: H&SO supplies the framework, Site Manager confirms it's in place on site, OCC files the paperwork.
- Catch a near-miss or observation and turn it into a tracked corrective action with one audit trail.
- Build a defensible "golden thread" of inspections, signatures, photos and timestamps.

## Pain points (current state)
- H&S evidence scattered across RAMsApp, SharePoint, paper toolbox-talk sheets, photos in WhatsApp.
- No central inspection/audit engine with photos, signatures, deadlines.
- Incidents and near-misses sit outside the corrective action loop.

## Involvement across workflows
- **Owner on:** 04 (H&S Site Mobilisation & Compliance — the framework, audits, inspections, incidents, corrective actions).
- **Approver on:** 03 (subcontractor compliance gate before award — H&S side), 06 (H&S inspections and observations on the live site), 08 (close-out H&S sign-off).
- **Read on:** 01 (drawings — for design risk review), 09 (portfolio H&S analytics).

## Permissions (coarse)
- Owner on inspection templates, audit templates, incident register, corrective action register, mobilisation checklist standards.
- Approver on H&S sign-off through any workflow.
- Read across all projects' H&S data.

## Devices
- Tablet primary (inspections / audits on site). Desktop for analytics and templates.
