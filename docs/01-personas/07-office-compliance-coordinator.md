# P07 — Office & Compliance Coordinator

**Type:** Internal
**Reports to:** Directors / FD
**Frequency on platform:** Daily
**Status:** Draft

## What OCC owns (tightened)

The admin and compliance backbone. Distinct from H&SO (H&S framework) and Site Manager (live site).

- Subcontractor onboarding records.
- Insurance / certificate / CIS / RAMS admin collection.
- Expiry tracking and chase.
- Filing and register maintenance.
- Document issue support to PM and QS.
- Compliance paperwork completeness.

## What OCC does **not** own
- Day-to-day site safety enforcement — that's Site Manager.
- The H&S framework, inspections or audits — that's H&SO.
- Live site operations.
- Commercial decision making.

## Goals
- Keep every subcontractor's insurance, certifications, RAMS and CIS current and visible at the point of award.
- Stop being the human reminder service — JPMS sends the expiry reminders, OCC reviews the exceptions.
- Keep the compliance paperwork register clean enough to defend against any tender evidence request.

## Pain points (current state)
- Subcontractor compliance scattered across Monday, SharePoint, Excel.
- Insurance / accreditation renewals tracked in Outlook calendar and chased manually.

## Involvement across workflows
- **Owner on:** 03 (Subcontractor Procurement & Onboarding — the onboarding-records slice).
- **Contributor on:** 04 (provides compliance documents that feed H&S mobilisation), 09 (admin metrics for portfolio reporting).
- **Read on:** 06, 08 (compliance status during live delivery and at close).

## Permissions (coarse)
- Owner on subcontractor master record, compliance document register, renewal reminders.
- Read on procurement so they can see who is being considered and check status before award.

## Devices
- Desktop primarily.
