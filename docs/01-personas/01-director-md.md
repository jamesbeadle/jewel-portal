# P01 — Director / MD

**Type:** Internal executive
**Reports to:** —
**Frequency on platform:** Daily
**Status:** Draft

## Goals
- See cashflow position and project portfolio status independently and in real time.
- Approve high-value commercial decisions in-system, with an audit trail.
- Catch margin erosion, risk concentration and at-risk projects early.

## Pain points (current state)
- Cashflow visibility lives in the FD's head until Excel is updated.
- Sign-offs scattered across email; no single audit trail.
- Portfolio-level patterns (win rate, overdue RFIs, defects backlog, subcontractor exposure) need building by hand each time someone asks.

## Involvement across workflows
- **Approver on:** 02 (tender sign-off), 03 (high-value award), 05 (high-value variations and NoDs), 06 (valuation approval), 07 (cashflow strategic decisions), 08 (final VAT outcome, retention release above threshold), 09 (portfolio thresholds and director-level exception escalations).
- **Read on:** every workflow — Directors see everything.

## Permissions (coarse)
- Read across all projects, all roles, all data.
- Approve high-value commercial decisions, valuations above threshold, the final VAT outcome, and director-level exceptions.
- Admin on users, roles, integrations.

## Devices
- Mobile + desktop. Primary mode is dashboards plus targeted approvals on mobile.
