# Business overview

Jewel Bespoke Build (JBB) is the Surrey super-prime residential construction division of Jewel Enterprises. JBB delivers high-end residential projects commissioned by architects, often for homeowner clients with significant value at stake.

## Who JBB serves
- **Architects / designers / consultants** — JBB's direct commissioning parties. They send tenders, approve RFIs and variations, and review valuations.
- **Clients / homeowners** — the end customer, often the person living in the resulting property. They approve selections, payments, Practical Completion and defects sign-off.
- **Subcontractors** — the field delivery partners JBB awards work to.

## What JPMS exists to do
JPMS runs the project lifecycle inside JBB so that:
- nothing important lives only in someone's inbox, WhatsApp, or spreadsheet;
- two business-critical outputs fall out of project data automatically — the Programme Valuation Report per claim period, and the cashflow forecast;
- the accountancy team can run AP, AR, payroll and VAT in their own tools (Xero, Brightpay) downstream without re-keying JPMS data;
- the Directors can see portfolio risk and margin in real time without asking.
